Learn to make Rice



  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Use the right ratio of water. Add 2
    parts water and 1 part rice to a large pot. ...
  3. Bring the water to a boil.
    Once it's boiling, add a big pinch of salt.
  4. Maintain a simmer. ...
  5. Cook without peeking or stirring...
  6. Let the rice rest covered. ...
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork.
  8. Wait for few Min & serve hot..

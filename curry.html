Learn to make Curry



  1. firstly heat 4 tsp oil and saute spices.
  2. saute 1 onion and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
  3. further add spices and 1 tsp salt. ...
  4. add tomato puree and cook well.
  5. now add roasted mix vegetables and mix well.
  6. add 1/2 cup water and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Wait for few Min & you can serve
